# Review Checklist V1

## Always check
- missing date
- missing amount
- unclear tax amount
- unreadable counterparty
- very blurry scan
- possible private use
- hospitality
- foreign software/API costs
- affiliate or commission payments
- unusually high amount

## Specific checks
### Hospitality
- participants present?
- occasion present?
- location identifiable?

### Hotel / travel
- business reason clear?
- breakfast or extras included?
- route or destination understandable?

### Software / API / SaaS
- provider location identifiable?
- domestic VAT shown or reverse charge more likely?
- invoice present, not just a payment confirmation?

### Vehicle costs
- date clear?
- amount clear?
- business use plausible?

## Review outcomes
- manually_checked
- needs_review
